What is Ilra CakeArt?

Ilra CakeArt is a cake store that specializes to create an amazing custom art of cakes. We use only the highest quality and healthy ingredients to make this beautiful and delicious cakes and our cakes are made to order.

We are helping people to make memorable and precious moment on their important event and we are commited to providing our customers with the best experience.

Why Choose Us?
Customizable Design Create your own custom cake with ease and freedom!
Affordable Cake Get high-quality cakes at affordable prices that are equivalent to those of large stores.
Healthy Cake Get healthy cakes made with natural ingredients and without preservatives!
Halal Cake Enjoy our cakes without any concern, because we are already halal certified by the government.
